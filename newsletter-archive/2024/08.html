Checkout Web Drop-in/Components v6 is now available!

After three successful years of v5, we are thrilled to introduce Web Drop-in/Components v6—a major upgrade that brings enhanced performance, new features, and a better developer experience. Here’s what’s new:

- Reduced bundle size with tree shaking for faster loading.
- New Bento design
- Support for Apple Pay Order Tracking
- 6 new localizations to better support your global users
- Improved developer experience
