Official Flutter Drop-in/Components v1 Release

We’re happy to announce the release of the official Flutter Drop-in/Components v1. Now, you can accept payments natively on both iOS and Android.

Key features:
  • Customizable Drop-in and standalone Components
  • Support for /sessions and the advanced flow
  • 3DSecure support
  • Client Side Encryption and standalone action handling for API-only customers

Visit our GitHub to discover the example app and find more information on supported payment methods on Adyen docs.

Get started >>

Adyen Hyvä Checkout Module: Expanded Support for Alternative Methods

Enhance your checkout experience with the Adyen Hyvä Checkout compatibility module. Now, you can use the features of the Hyvä theme and offer your shoppers a wide range of payment options.

New features include:
  • Support for cards, wallets, and other alternative payment methods
  • Option for shoppers to pay in installments
  • Donation options during payment
  • Secure storage of payment details for faster checkouts

Learn more >>

Introducing New Demo Store Apps for Android and iOS

In addition to the MyStore demo for Web, we now offer MyStore apps for both iOS and Android. These apps showcase the capabilities of our mobile SDKs for processing in-app payments and include:
  • A product overview screen
  • A cart screen
  • A checkout screen using the mobile Drop-in integration
  • A settings screen to change the country/currency to try different local payment methods.

Try out the new MyStore demo app for Android and iOS (mobile only) >>

P630 Terminal: Now Live Across Multiple Regions

The P630 Terminal now supports Test and Live use across the EU, UK, US, and CA. The P630, an all-in-one Android device, is designed to process payments and run business applications seamlessly. It serves as a PCI 6.x alternative to the P400Plus. The terminal uses the same cables and accessories as the P400, ensuring an easy transition.

Why choose P630?
  • All-in-one Android functionality
  • Self-boarding capabilities
  • Fully customizable

Learn more about P630 Terminal >>

New Tipping Flexibility: Specify Tip Amounts Separate from Total Transactions

Starting with firmware 103, our terminals now support tipping on amounts separate from the total transaction.

Key benefits:
  • Specify tip amount independently of the total transaction
  • Perfect for prepayments and non-tippable items
  • Improved customer satisfaction and increased tipping potential
