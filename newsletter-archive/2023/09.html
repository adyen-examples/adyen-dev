Adyen Checkout Web (Drop-in/Components) obtained a flawless accessibility score
In this month's featured update, we'd like to share that the Adyen Checkout Web has completed an external accessibility review with Level Access and we obtained a flawless Level AA rating ensuring that Web Drop-in and Components v5.49.0 or later are accessible to the widest possible audience.
