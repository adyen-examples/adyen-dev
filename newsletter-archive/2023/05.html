May Developer Newsletter
Hi folks,

Here's our Developer’s Newsletter for May: blogs, conferences and new releases of our libraries and plugins. But first of all: nice to meet you!

I am Gerda Užubalytė, the most recent upgrade of Developer Relations team (DevRel v5.0). Wondering how it is possible for this team to be even better? One word - data. Starting my journey at Adyen as Data Analyst, I am ready to crunch numbers and help the team as well as our tech community by translating data into actions and understanding their needs better. Working for different e-commerce scale-ups in the past 4 years, I gained experience in making an impact using data-driven insights. Data will help us provide better content & build even stronger developers’ community outside Adyen. Looking forward to working with you and make this journey a fun adventure!

Past Events
April 11, PostgresQL Meetup Amsterdam
We hosted the PostgreSQL NL meetup at our HQ in Amsterdam. Our own Derk van Veen talked about partitioning, pros/cons and lessons learnt. Then Bruce Momjian (co-founder and core team member of the PostgreSQL Global Development Group) led a talk about deploying databases in the cloud. The meeting ended with a fireside chat about the state of the beloved database.
KotlinConf 2023
KotlinConf 2023 was amazing. It was great to be there to meet the community and talk about all things Kotlin (which we love at Adyen). I hope you have visited our booth and grabbed some swags, otherwise don't miss it next time.
Visit event ›
April 17, Data on Kubernetes Meetup
It was a pleasure to host the DoK community and discuss about running databases on Kubernetes. We heard the experience of projects run by the Dutch government (by Sebastiaan Mannem) as well as a talk covering how the container hosting platform team deals with local persistence at scale (by Tor Bendiksen and Luuk Stolk).
