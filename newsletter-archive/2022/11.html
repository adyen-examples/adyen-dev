Past Events
12-13 October, Adyen Tech Event
On Oct 12th and 13th we hosted in Amsterdam, at the beautiful SugarCity venue, our yearly Adyen Tech Event with all our global tech, engineering and product teams.

In the 2 days we packed 60+ sessions, technical workshops, customer panels, a chat with the board, our own Tech Awards and an amazing closing party.
Key take-aways:
  • 1500 attendes from our (global) engineering teams got together to learn, share and brainstorm
  • we celebrated the achievements of an important year for Adyen as well as looking back at what we learned and what we can do better
  • we looked at the goals, opportunities and challenges in front of us to stay in control and plan our future
